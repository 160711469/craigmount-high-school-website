Craigmount's history

craigmount highschool are a school with a vast range of stories and legacies. You will find some intersting information down below

Opening its doors in August 1970, Craigmount High School welcomed in cohorts of first, second and third years to the new school from neighbouring Tynecastle, Forresters, Craigroyston amongst many others. Since then, the school has acted as a hub for the surrounding community, whether it is through hosting charity fundraisers, to drama productions or as a centre for local groups to congregate.
