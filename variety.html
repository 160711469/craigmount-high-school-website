Craigmount Subject Variety

When attending Craigmount, pupils in S2 will make a decision aboout what subjects they would like to take in school

You pick from 6 different collumns...

expressive arts which inclues of art & design, dance, drama, media studies, music and music technology

The next collumn is languages which consists of French, German and Spanish

collomn number three is science where you can choose from Biology, Physics, Chemistry and general science

next up is Social Subjects where you can pick from buisness enterprise, buisness managment, classical studies, geography, history, modern studies, philosophy & moral studies and lastly people & society

collumn number 5 is technologies which has many options such as, accounting, administration & IT, buisness enterprise, buisness managment, computing science, design engineering & construct, engineering science, food tech, graphic communications, media studies, music technology, photography, physics and practical woodwork

the last collumn is additional choice where students have a free pick of just about every subject Craigmount has to offer
